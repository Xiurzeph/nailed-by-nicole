Our Premium Nail Services

At Nailed By C. Nicole, we offer comprehensive nail services designed to pamper and beautify your hands and feet. Our expert nail technicians use only the highest quality products and techniques to ensure your luxury nail artistry experience exceeds expectations. Whether you're looking for a relaxing spa day or preparing for a special occasion, our professional nail care services in Clinton, Maryland will leave you feeling confident and beautiful.

Luxury Manicure

Our signature luxury manicure service treats your hands to professional nail care that will leave your nails looking stunning and your skin feeling soft and rejuvenated. This comprehensive nail artistry service includes precise nail shaping, expert cuticle care, relaxing hand massage, and your choice of premium polish application for a flawless finish.

Relaxing Pedicure

Relax and unwind with our professional pedicure service as you immerse your feet in a soothing, aromatic footbath. Our relaxing pedicure will pamper your feet with meticulous nail trimming, expert cuticle work, exfoliation, callus removal, and a therapeutic foot massage. Complete your spa experience with your favorite polish shade or choose a natural buffed shine.

Gel Polish

Indulge in the long-lasting beauty of our professional gel polish application, known for its exceptional durability and brilliant shine. Our gel polish nail service includes expert nail shaping, cuticle care, and the perfect coating of your chosen gel color for chip-free, glossy nails that maintain their beauty for weeks.

Expert Fill-ins

Maintain the beauty of your acrylic or gel nails with our expert fill-in service. Our skilled nail technicians carefully blend the new growth with the existing nail enhancements, ensuring a seamless and natural-looking result. Your nails will be professionally reshaped, buffed, and polished to perfection.

Full Set Application

Transform your nails with our professional full set application service, ideal for those looking to add length and strength to their natural nails. Choose from a variety of nail enhancements, including acrylic or gel extensions, customized to suit your desired shape and style. Our skilled technicians provide precise application for beautiful, long-lasting results.
