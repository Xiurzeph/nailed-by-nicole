Our Premium Services

Luxury Manicure

Treat your hands to a luxurious manicure that will leave your nails looking stunning and your skin feeling soft and rejuvenated. Includes shaping, cuticle care, hand massage, and your choice of polish.

Relaxing Pedicure

Relax and unwind as you immerse your feet in a soothing, aromatic footbath. Our pedicure will pamper your feet with meticulous care, exfoliation, callus removal, and relaxing massage.

Gel Polish

Indulge in the long-lasting beauty of gel polish, known for its durability and brilliant shine. Our gel polish application includes nail shaping, cuticle care, and the perfect coating for chip-free, glossy nails that last for weeks.

Expert Fill-ins

Maintain the beauty of your acrylic or gel nails with our expert fill-in service. We carefully blend new growth with existing enhancements for a seamless, natural-looking result.

Full Set Application

Transform your nails with our full set service, ideal for adding length and strength. Choose from acrylic or gel extensions, customized to suit your desired shape and style.
